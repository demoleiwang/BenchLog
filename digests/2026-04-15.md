**New entries (5, all agent category):**

1. **Mind2Web** — [arXiv:2306.06070](https://arxiv.org/abs/2306.06070) — OSU NLP, June 2023, NeurIPS'23 Spotlight. 2,000+ tasks across 137 real websites × 31 domains. Foundational web-agent benchmark; later ancestors of WebArena / VisualWebArena / Online-Mind2Web.

5. **ScreenSpot-Pro** — [arXiv:2504.07981](https://arxiv.org/abs/2504.07981) — Kaixin Li et al., April 2025, ACM Multimedia 2025. Pure GUI grounding: given screenshot + instruction, predict (x,y) pixel. 23 professional apps × 3 OSes. Best base model 18.9%; ScreenSeekeR search method 48.1%.
